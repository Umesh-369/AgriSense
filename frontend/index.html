The Challenge

Why Traditional Methods Fail

Manual irrigation and guesswork lead to significant problems in agriculture

💸

Water Waste

Over 40% of irrigation water is wasted due to improper timing and excessive watering without real data.

🥀

Crop Damage

Both over-watering and under-watering cause root diseases, nutrient loss, and reduced crop yields.

⏰

Time Consuming

Manual soil checking requires constant attention and physical presence in the field.

🔮

No Predictions

Without historical data, farmers cannot predict trends or optimize their irrigation schedules.

How AgriSense Works

From sensor to dashboard in four simple steps

1

Data Collection

Soil sensors capture real-time moisture data, while farmers capture images of potentially diseased crops using their smartphones.

📡

Data is sent to our secure cloud where Google Gemini AI analyzes crop images for diseases and algorithms process soil data.

3
